Tipsy Mix


Like Hand Grenade

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1 1/2 oz grain alcohol
  • 1 1/2 oz melon liqueur
  • 1 1/2 oz rum
  • 1 1/2 oz vodka

Recipe

Stir ingredients together in a collins glass filled with ice cubes. Add water and sugar if desired, to taste, and serve in "Collins Glass".
