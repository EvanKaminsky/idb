One of our favorite, classic cocktails, and the Dude's, The White Russian. To mix a White Russian use the following recipe instructions: Pour Kahlúa and vodka into old fashioned glass filled with ice. Float fresh cream on the top. Stir in slowly, and serve. For a lighter drink, the cream can be substituted for milk. Enjoy your White Russian!
